# Moje 357

Goals of this project: 
1. Live preview of songs being played on [Radio 357](https://radio357.pl)
2. Playlist of all songs being played
3. Authentication with Spotify account
4. Spotify features: playlists, liked songs
5. Stats of songs being played on air

Listen to [Radio 357](https://radio357.pl) and support them on [Patronite](https://patronite.pl/radio357).
